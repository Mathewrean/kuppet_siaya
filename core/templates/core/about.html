About The Branch

Serving Teachers Across Siaya County

KUPPET Siaya Branch exists to protect teacher welfare, strengthen professional unity, and provide dependable support to members across the county.

The branch works as a trusted institutional platform for communication, advocacy, welfare coordination, and program delivery. Through organised leadership structures and member-focused initiatives, we help keep teachers informed, represented, and connected to branch services.

Within this portal, members and stakeholders can follow official branch updates, review ongoing programmes, access gallery highlights, and reach the branch through a clear digital contact channel.

Explore the branch profile, meet the committee teams, and learn more about the initiatives that continue to shape KUPPET Siaya’s work on behalf of teachers.
