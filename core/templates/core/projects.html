Branch Projects And Initiatives

KUPPET Siaya Branch runs structured initiatives that respond to welfare, investment, and institutional support priorities for members.

These programmes strengthen the branch’s ability to serve teachers beyond routine administration. Each initiative is designed to meet a specific need, whether that is social welfare, enterprise development, or the expansion of branch facilities and service capacity.

Current focus areas

  • Building Better Future (BBF) for welfare support and member protection.
  • Business Unit Society (BUS) for branch enterprise and sustainability initiatives.
  • KUPPET Center development for infrastructure and service delivery.
