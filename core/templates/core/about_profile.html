Branch Profile

Institutional Profile

KUPPET Siaya Branch provides organised representation and welfare support for teachers while promoting accountability, service, and professional solidarity.

Our Role

The branch coordinates member engagement, welfare structures, official communication, and programme implementation at county level. It also serves as an accessible point of contact for union matters affecting teachers within Siaya.

Our Focus

Our work centres on advocacy, branch administration, member support, and the strengthening of initiatives that improve the day-to-day experience of teachers and their families.

Our Commitment

We remain committed to responsive leadership, transparent communication, and practical systems that help members access services with confidence.
