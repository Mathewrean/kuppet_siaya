KUPPET Center

The KUPPET Center project reflects the branch’s long-term investment in physical infrastructure that can support services, meetings, and institutional growth.

As the branch strengthens its operations, the center remains an important part of creating a stable and recognisable home for programme coordination and member-facing activity.

Project objectives

  • Provide space for branch coordination and organised service delivery.
  • Support official meetings, training, and member engagement activities.
  • Build a durable institutional asset for KUPPET Siaya Branch.
