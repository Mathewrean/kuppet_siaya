Business Unit Society (BUS)

BUS provides the branch with a framework for enterprise-oriented initiatives that can strengthen institutional sustainability over time.

The programme helps the branch organise business-related opportunities in a structured way, supporting long-term planning while remaining aligned with member interests and branch priorities.

Core value to the branch

  • Supports strategic financial thinking beyond routine administration.
  • Creates room for carefully managed income-generating activity.
  • Improves institutional resilience through organised business oversight.

Need more information?

The branch can provide official guidance on BUS priorities, governance, and approved participation pathways.
