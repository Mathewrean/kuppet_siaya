BUS — Branch Transport (Bus Services)

BUS is the branch's transport service programme, coordinating passenger transport services and journeys on behalf of members (school trips, branch logistics and member travel).

The BUS programme manages vehicle scheduling, fares, and logistics to support branch activities and member travel needs. It covers trip coordination, safety procedures, and approved transport services operated under the branch's oversight.

Core value to the branch

  • Provides reliable transport options for branch events and member travel.
  • Ensures trips and logistics are coordinated with safety and compliance in mind.
  • Supports managed revenue for transport operations where approved by the branch.

Need more information?

The branch can provide official guidance on BUS priorities, governance, and approved participation pathways.
