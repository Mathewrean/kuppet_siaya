Building Better Future (BBF)

BBF is the branch welfare initiative that helps members access structured support through regular contributions and shared responsibility.

The programme is designed to strengthen member welfare by creating a dependable support mechanism around times of need. It helps the branch respond more effectively while reinforcing the value of collective contribution.

Why BBF matters

  • Creates a predictable welfare framework for members.
  • Supports branch responsiveness through organised contributions.
  • Encourages long-term participation in member-focused support systems.

Non-member contribution option

Stakeholders who are not yet enrolled can still support the programme through the approved BBF payment pathway.
